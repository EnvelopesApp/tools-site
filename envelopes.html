A calmer budgeting loop

Three steps you can repeat every payday.

Envelopes stays close to the classic cash-envelope idea: money comes in, you give it jobs, then you spend from the jobs you already funded.

  1. 1
    Add your accounts

    Enter your checking, savings, credit, or loan balances. No bank passwords required.

  2. 2
    Fill your envelopes

    Move Ready to Assign money into the categories that need it this month.

  3. 3
    Log spending

    Record transactions as they happen and keep an honest view of what is still available.

Private by default

No bank linking, no ads, no third-party analytics in the app.

Your budget is stored on your device. If you sign in, Envelopes syncs your budget through the Envelopes cloud service so your devices can match. The app never asks for bank credentials.
